About The Activity Catalog (V1)

Welcome to the first iteration of the Community for Rigor Activity Catalog. Here we’ll be sharing content developed by the CENTER team such as activities, interactive visuals, and any sort of digital element that may come in handy during unit production. The goal is that the content here may provide inspiration for activity design, as well as provide starting points from which future activities may be built off of.

Keep in mind that most of the current content here is a prototype or proof-of-concept we’ve built in the last few weeks. We will gradually be adding new content and updating this page over the course of the project.

Note: Some of the content here will not be fully functional for security reasons (currently just the LLM Feedback Tool). For full functionality or technical issues, reach out to Jack Liddy for a functional link.
